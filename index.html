What Are Full Arch Dental Implants?

Full arch dental implants replace an entire upper or lower arch of teeth with a permanently fixed, natural-looking prosthesis supported by dental implants.

1

Assessment & Planning

Comprehensive examination including 3D CT scans to precisely plan your treatment. We assess bone quality, discuss your goals, and create a personalized treatment plan.

2

Implant Placement

Strategic placement of 4-6 dental implants per arch to support your new teeth. Using advanced techniques, we position implants at optimal angles for maximum stability.

3

Immediate Temporary Teeth

Walk out the same day with a full set of functional temporary teeth. You won't spend a day without teeth‚Äîeat soft foods and smile with confidence while you heal.

4

Final Beautiful Smile

After 3-6 months of healing, receive your custom-crafted final zirconia ceramic teeth. Designed to match your facial features, these teeth look completely natural and last for decades.

Your Journey to New Teeth

From your first consultation to your final smile, here's what to expect.

1

Free Consultation

Meet with Dr Will to discuss your concerns, examine your teeth, and explore your options. We'll take initial scans and photos, answer all your questions, and provide honest advice. No obligation, no pressure.

2

Digital Planning

3D CT scans and digital treatment planning allow us to design your treatment with precision. You'll see visualizations of your expected outcome before we begin.

3

Implant Surgery Day

Your implants are placed and provisional teeth fitted the same day. Walk out with a new smile. Most patients are pleasantly surprised by how comfortable they feel.

4

Healing Period (3-6 months)

Your implants integrate with your jaw while you wear comfortable provisional teeth. Regular check-ups ensure everything is progressing well.

5

Final Zirconia Ceramic Teeth

Your permanent, custom-crafted teeth are fitted. Designed for beauty, function, and longevity. These are your teeth for life.

6

Lifelong Care

Regular maintenance visits (usually twice yearly) ensure your implants last for decades. We're here to support you for life.
