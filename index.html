Your Journey to a New Smile

From your first consultation to your beautiful new teeth, here's what to expect at every step.

1

Free Consultation

Meet with Dr Will or Dr Sharry to discuss your goals, concerns, and options. We'll answer all your questions. No obligation, no pressure.

2

Digital Planning

3D scans and imaging allow us to plan your treatment with precision and show you the expected outcome.

3

Implant Surgery Day

Your implants are placed and provisional teeth fitted the same day. Walk out with a new smile.

4

Healing Period

Over 3-6 months, your implants integrate with your jaw while you wear comfortable provisional teeth.

5

Final Teeth

Your permanent, custom-crafted zirconia ceramic teeth are fitted. Designed for beauty, function, and longevity.

6

Lifelong Care

Regular maintenance visits ensure your implants last for decades. We're here for life.
