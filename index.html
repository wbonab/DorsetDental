Your Journey to Fixed Teeth

A clear, straightforward process from consultation to your new smile.

1

Free Consultation

Meet with us to discuss your concerns, explore your options, and receive a personalized treatment plan with transparent pricing.

2

Advanced Planning

3D CT scans and digital planning ensure precise implant placement for optimal results and comfort.

3

Implant Placement

Implants are placed using guided surgery techniques. You'll leave with a temporary fixed bridge the same day.

4

Healing Period

Over 3-6 months, implants integrate with your bone. You'll wear comfortable temporary teeth throughout.

5

Final Teeth Fitted

Your custom-crafted permanent teeth are fitted—designed for beauty, function, and durability.

6

Ongoing Care

Regular check-ups and professional cleanings ensure your implants last for decades.
