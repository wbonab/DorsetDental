Treatment Options

Implant Services

We provide comprehensive dental implant treatment from single tooth replacement to complete arch rehabilitation. All cases are planned using 3D CBCT imaging to ensure optimal outcomes.

Treatment 01

Full Arch Rehabilitation

Complete upper or lower arch replacement using fixed implant-supported prosthetics. Suitable for patients with multiple failing teeth or complete edentulism.

Treatment 02

Dentures to Fixed

Transition from removable dentures to permanently fixed implant-retained teeth. Restore confidence and natural function.

Treatment 03

Single & Multiple Implants

Replace missing teeth without affecting adjacent healthy teeth. Natural-looking ceramic crowns on titanium implants.
