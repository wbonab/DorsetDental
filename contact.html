Frequently Asked Questions

Is the consultation really free?

Yes, completely free with no obligation. You'll receive a comprehensive examination, 3D CT scan, and written treatment plan at no cost.

How long does a consultation take?

Allow 45-60 minutes for your consultation. This gives us enough time to assess your situation thoroughly and answer all your questions.

Do I need a referral?

No referral is necessary. You can book directly with us.

What should I bring to my consultation?

Just yourself! If you have any recent dental records or x-rays, feel free to bring them, but it's not essential.

Can I bring someone with me?

Absolutely. Many patients find it helpful to bring a family member or friend for support.

What if I can't make it during regular hours?

We offer some Saturday appointments by arrangement. Please mention your availability when booking and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
