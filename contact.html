Frequently Asked Questions About Consultations

Is the consultation really free?

Yes, absolutely. Your initial consultation, including clinical examination and 3D imaging, is completely free of charge with no obligation to proceed.

How long does a consultation take?

Allow approximately 45 minutes to an hour. We don't rush‚Äîwe want to give you the time you need to understand your options and ask questions.

What should I bring to my consultation?

Please bring:

  • A list of any medications you're currently taking
  • Details of any medical conditions
  • Your dental records if you have them (though not essential)
  • A list of questions you'd like to ask

Will I need to make a decision on the day?

Absolutely not. We'll provide you with a detailed treatment plan and cost breakdown to take home. Take all the time you need to consider your options.

Can I bring someone with me?

Yes, we encourage you to bring a family member or friend for support if you'd like. It's helpful to have someone with you who can help remember information and ask questions.

Do you offer consultations outside regular hours?

Yes, we offer some early morning and Saturday appointments. Please mention your availability when booking and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
