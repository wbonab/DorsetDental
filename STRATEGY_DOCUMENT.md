## 6. GDC COMPLIANCE & LEGAL

### 6.1 UK Dental Advertising Guidelines

**GDC Standards for Dental Advertising:**

✓ **Be Accurate:** Don't make misleading or unsubstantiated claims
✓ **Be Responsible:** Don't exploit patient vulnerabilities or anxieties
✓ **Be Honest:** Don't make unrealistic promises or guarantees
✓ **Be Professional:** Maintain dignity of profession

**Compliant Language:**
- ✓ "High success rates" (with citation)
- ✗ "100% success rate" (unrealistic guarantee)
- ✓ "Can last 20+ years with proper care"
- ✗ "Will last forever" (absolute claim)
- ✓ "Most patients experience minimal discomfort"
- ✗ "Completely painless procedure" (subjective, may not be true for all)
- ✓ "Results may vary depending on individual circumstances"
- ✗ "Everyone will have perfect teeth" (guarantee)

**Required Disclaimers:**
- Individual results may vary
- Treatment carries risks (specify and discuss)
- Not all patients are suitable candidates
- Consultation required to determine suitability

### 6.2 Before/After Photography

**If Using Patient Photos:**
- Obtain written consent specifically for website use
- Ensure photos are the practice's own cases
- Don't alter images to mislead
- Include disclaimer: "Results may vary"
- Store consent forms securely (GDPR)
- Allow patients to withdraw consent

**Alternative to Real Photos:**
- Use stock photography (clearly not representing actual patients)
- Use illustrations or diagrams
- Focus on educational content rather than results

## 10. RISK MITIGATION

### 10.1 Potential Challenges

**Challenge 1: High Competition**
*Solution:* Differentiate through Harvard training, prosthodontic specialization, and patient-centered approach. Focus on quality over volume.

**Challenge 2: High Cost of Acquisition**
*Solution:* Focus on conversion rate optimization to improve ROI. Invest in retention and referrals to reduce reliance on new patient acquisition.

**Challenge 3: Long Sales Cycle**
*Solution:* Implement nurture email campaigns. Provide valuable education content to stay top-of-mind. Offer flexible consultation times.

**Challenge 4: Negative Reviews**
*Solution:* Actively solicit reviews from happy patients. Respond professionally to all reviews. Have a clear complaints procedure.

**Challenge 5: Algorithm Changes**
*Solution:* Diversify traffic sources (organic, paid, social, referral). Don't rely solely on SEO. Build email list for direct communication.

**Challenge 6: GDC Compliance Issues**
*Solution:* Regular review of all marketing materials. Consult with dental defense organization. Err on side of caution with claims.

### 10.2 Crisis Management

**Reputation Management Plan:**
- Monitor online reviews daily
- Respond to negative reviews within 24 hours
- Have templated responses prepared
- Escalate serious complaints immediately
- Never argue publicly with patients
- Offer to discuss offline

**Website Downtime Plan:**
- Have backup hosting provider identified
- Maintain offline backups of website
- Use status page to communicate issues
- Have phone number prominently displayed on error pages
