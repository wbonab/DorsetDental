Our Practice

Postgraduate-trained expertise in dental implant rehabilitation with a focus on digital planning, precision placement, and premium zirconia restorations.

Dr. Will Bonab BDS

Bournemouth Dental Implants is led by a clinician with dedicated postgraduate training in prosthodontics and implant dentistry. Dr. Will Bonab combines expertise from King's College London and Harvard University to deliver evidence-based, digitally-planned implant treatment.

With a focus on full arch rehabilitation, complex prosthetic cases, and digital treatment planning, every patient receives comprehensive assessment using 3D CBCT imaging and precision-guided implant placement.
