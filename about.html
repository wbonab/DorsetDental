About Bournemouth Implants

Bournemouth Implants is a specialist dental implant service focused exclusively on full arch rehabilitation. We help patients across Bournemouth, Poole, Christchurch, and throughout Dorset transition from dentures or failing teeth to permanently fixed dental implants.

Our approach combines advanced training, cutting-edge technology, and genuine patient care to deliver exceptional, life-changing results.

Our Philosophy

We believe that everyone deserves to eat, smile, and live with confidence. Dental problems—whether from years of wearing dentures, failing teeth, or dental anxiety—shouldn't hold you back from enjoying life.

Our mission is simple: to provide expert, compassionate care that transforms not just smiles, but lives.

Our Expertise

Full arch dental implant treatment is complex and requires specialized knowledge, training, and experience. At Bournemouth Implants, you benefit from:

  • Advanced Training: Postgraduate prosthodontic training at Harvard School of Dental Medicine, focused on complex implant rehabilitation
  • Specialized Focus: We concentrate exclusively on full arch cases, allowing us to refine our expertise
  • Digital Workflows: 3D planning software and guided surgery for precision placement
  • Comprehensive Care: Everything from planning through final restoration managed in-house
  • Proven Track Record: Years of successful full arch cases across Bournemouth and Dorset
  • Ongoing Education: Commitment to staying current with latest techniques and technology

Why Choose Us?

When you're considering full arch dental implants, choosing the right practice matters. Here's what sets us apart:

1. Specialist Training & Experience

Full arch implant treatment is not routine dentistry. It requires advanced training in prosthodontics, implant surgery, and complex rehabilitation. Our lead clinician completed postgraduate prosthodontic training at Harvard School of Dental Medicine, one of the world's leading institutions for advanced dental education.

2. Exclusive Focus on Full Arch Cases

We don't do general dentistry. We focus exclusively on full arch implant rehabilitation. This specialization means we've developed streamlined processes, refined techniques, and accumulated extensive experience in this specific area.

3. Advanced Technology

We invest heavily in technology that improves outcomes:

  • 3D CT Scanning: Detailed analysis of your bone and anatomy
  • Digital Planning Software: Virtual surgery before actual surgery
  • Guided Surgery: Surgical guides for precise implant placement
  • Digital Impressions: More comfortable and accurate than traditional impressions
  • CAD/CAM Design: Computer-designed teeth for optimal fit and aesthetics

4. Transparent & Honest Approach

We believe in giving you all the information you need to make an informed decision:

  • Comprehensive written treatment plans
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden costs
  • Realistic expectations about recovery and results
  • Honest assessment of whether you're a suitable candidate
  • Time to consider your options without pressure

5. Patient-Centered Care

We understand that considering dental implants can be daunting. Our approach is built around your needs:

  • Unhurried consultations where all questions are welcome
  • Clear communication at every stage
  • Sedation options available for anxious patients
  • Emergency contact access during recovery
  • Comprehensive aftercare and support

Who We Help

We work with patients from all walks of life who share a common goal: to regain the confidence and function that comes with fixed teeth. Our patients include:

  • Long-term denture wearers ready for a permanent solution
  • People with multiple failing or missing teeth

  • Active individuals who want the freedom to eat and live normally
  • Anyone who's had enough of the limitations of removable dentures

Regardless of your situation, if you're unhappy with your teeth and considering a change, we're here to help.
