Cost Factors

What Influences Price?

Several clinical factors determine your exact treatment cost.

Number of Implants

Full arch treatment typically requires 4-6 implants per arch. The exact number depends on your bone quality, jaw anatomy, and desired outcome. More implants may be recommended for optimal load distribution and long-term stability.

Bone Grafting Requirements

If you have experienced significant bone loss, grafting procedures may be necessary before or during implant placement. This increases treatment scope and cost but ensures optimal implant positioning and long-term success.

Extraction Procedures

Patients with failing teeth requiring removal will need extraction procedures as part of treatment. Complex extractions or multiple teeth requiring surgical removal will affect the overall treatment timeline and cost.

Temporary Prosthetics

During the healing phase, you may require temporary restorations. The type and complexity of temporary prosthesis needed varies between cases and influences total treatment investment.

Premium Zirconia Ceramic Teeth

All final restorations are fabricated from high-grade zirconia ceramic - the premium material for fixed dental prosthetics.

Exceptional Strength

Zirconia is one of the strongest dental ceramics available, providing excellent durability for long-term function.

Natural Aesthetics

Superior translucency and shade matching creates natural-looking teeth that blend seamlessly with your smile.

Stain Resistant

Highly resistant to discolouration from food, drink, and tobacco - maintaining appearance over time.

Biocompatible

Excellent tissue compatibility minimises soft tissue irritation and supports healthy gum integration.

Colour Stability

Unlike traditional materials, zirconia maintains its colour indefinitely without yellowing or fading.

Metal-Free

All-ceramic construction eliminates concerns about metal sensitivity and provides superior aesthetics.
