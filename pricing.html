Why Do Costs Vary?

Several factors influence the final cost of your treatment:

  • Number of Implants: Most cases use 4-6 implants per arch. Some patients may need more for optimal stability.
  • Complexity of Surgery: Cases requiring bone grafting or sinus lifts cost more than straightforward placements.
  • Type of Final Teeth: Premium materials (like zirconia) cost more than standard acrylic, but offer superior aesthetics and durability.
  • Upper vs. Lower Arch: Upper arch treatment is sometimes more complex due to bone density and sinus anatomy.
  • Number of Arches: Treating both upper and lower arches together may offer cost savings.

During your consultation, we'll assess your individual situation and provide an accurate, personalized quote.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. We work with trusted finance providers to offer 0% interest-free plans (6-12 months) and extended payment plans (up to 5 years). Most applications receive instant decisions.

Are there any hidden costs?

No. Your written quote includes everything from consultation through to your final teeth and first year of follow-up care. The only additional costs would be if you choose optional extras like sedation.

Is the consultation really free?

Yes, completely free with no obligation. You'll receive a full examination, 3D CT scan, and written treatment plan at no cost.

Can I pay in installments without finance?

Yes. You can pay a deposit to secure your treatment date, with the balance due before or on your surgery day, or use one of our finance plans.

What happens if something goes wrong after treatment?

All necessary follow-up care in the first year is included. If complications arise due to the treatment, we'll address them at no additional cost. Your treatment plan will detail our warranty terms.

Is treatment covered by insurance?

Private dental insurance policies vary significantly. Some offer partial coverage for implants. We recommend checking with your insurer. We can provide documentation to support any claims.
