Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies on our website

Last Updated: February 8, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Bournemouth Dental Implants ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website at bournemouthimplants.com ("Website"). By using our Website, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

Cookies typically contain:

  • The name of the website that created the cookie
  • A unique identifier
  • An expiration date
  • Information about your browsing activity

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure our Website functions properly
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you use our Website
  • Improve your browsing experience
  • Provide relevant information and content
  • Measure the effectiveness of our website

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

Purpose: Essential for the Website to function properly
Examples: Security, navigation, basic functionality
Can be disabled: No (Website won't work without them)

These cookies are essential for you to browse the Website and use its features. Without these cookies, services like secure areas and contact forms cannot be provided.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
session_id Maintains your session while browsing Session (deleted when browser closes)
cookie_consent Remembers your cookie preferences 1 year

3.2 Performance Cookies

Purpose: Collect information about how you use our Website
Examples: Google Analytics (if implemented)
Can be disabled: Yes

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our Website, such as which pages are visited most often. This data is aggregated and anonymous. We use this information to improve how our Website works.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics - distinguishes users 2 years
_gid Google Analytics - distinguishes users 24 hours
_gat Google Analytics - throttles request rate 1 minute

Note: Google Analytics cookies are only set if you consent to performance cookies.

3.3 Functionality Cookies

Purpose: Remember your preferences and provide enhanced features
Examples: Language preferences, font size, display settings
Can be disabled: Yes

These cookies allow the Website to remember choices you make (such as your preferred language or region) and provide enhanced, more personalized features.

3.4 Targeting/Advertising Cookies

Purpose: Deliver relevant advertisements and marketing
Examples: Google Ads, Facebook Pixel (if implemented)
Can be disabled: Yes

Current Status: We do not currently use advertising cookies on our Website.

In the future, we may use these cookies to:

  • Show you relevant advertisements based on your interests
  • Limit the number of times you see an advertisement
  • Measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns

If we implement advertising cookies, we will update this policy and seek your consent.

  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences

You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in our footer.

6. What Happens If You Disable Cookies?

If you disable cookies:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Some features of our Website may not function properly
  • Performance Cookies: We won't be able to improve the Website based on usage data
  • Functionality Cookies: The Website won't remember your preferences
  • Targeting Cookies: You may see less relevant advertisements (if applicable)

You can still browse our Website and contact us for consultations without cookies, though some features may be limited.

7.3 Session Storage

Temporary storage that is cleared when you close your browser.
