§ 01What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file that a website stores on your device when you visit. Cookies make websites work more efficiently and allow the operator to understand, in aggregate, how the site is used.

We use as few cookies as possible. Where we use non-essential cookies, we ask for your consent first.

§ 02Cookies used on this site

The table below summarises every cookie that may be set when you visit bournemouthimplants.com. Categories follow the ICO's classification.

§ 03Strictly necessary cookies

These cookies are required for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually set in response to actions you take, such as filling in the booking form.

  • bdi_session — preserves your place in the multi-step booking form. Expires when you close the browser.
  • bdi_consent — records your cookie-consent choice. Expires after 12 months.
  • XSRF-TOKEN — used to protect form submissions from cross-site request forgery. Session only.

§ 04Analytics cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand which pages are visited and how patients move through the booking flow. They are only set if you accept them on the consent banner.

  • _ga, _ga_* — Google Analytics 4. Used to distinguish unique visitors and track aggregate usage. IP addresses are truncated before storage. Expires after 13 months.
  • _clck, _clsk — Microsoft Clarity. Records anonymised interaction patterns to help us improve the website. Expires after 12 months / one day respectively.

We do not use advertising or retargeting cookies, and we do not share analytics data with third parties for marketing.

§ 05Managing your preferences

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie preferences" link in the website footer, or by clearing the bdi_consent cookie from your browser. The next page load will then prompt you for a new choice.

§ 06Browser controls

All modern browsers allow you to block or delete cookies on a per-site or global basis:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Blocking strictly-necessary cookies will prevent the booking form from working correctly, but will not stop you from browsing the rest of the site.

§ 07Changes to this policy

We will update this page if the set of cookies in use changes. The "Effective" date at the top of the page indicates the most recent material revision.
