§ Signs it's time

Your dentures are telling you something.

Most patients come to us having worn dentures for a decade or more. They're not here because of vanity. They're here because the denture has started to cost them something — confidence, nutrition, sleep, or all three.

If several of these sound familiar, an implant consultation is likely worthwhile.

01
You carry adhesive in your pocket.
Creams and pastes that were meant to be occasional have become a daily routine.
02
Certain foods have been quietly dropped.
Apples, crusty bread, steak — items you used to enjoy and now avoid to keep the denture in place.
03
Your lower denture moves when you talk.
A well-made upper denture can hold itself; lowers almost always need implants to be stable.
04
You've developed sore spots or ulcers.
Bone loss under the denture has changed the fit, and no amount of reline can fix it.
05
You don't laugh the way you used to.
The small, self-conscious adjustments add up over years. Partners tend to notice before patients do.
