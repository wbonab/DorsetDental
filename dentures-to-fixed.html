The Conversion Process

Your Journey to Fixed Teeth

Assessment Phase

We begin with comprehensive evaluation:

  • Clinical examination of current dentures and oral health
  • CBCT 3D imaging to assess bone volume and quality
  • Digital treatment planning to determine implant positions
  • Discussion of treatment options and timeline
  • Financial planning and cost breakdown

Treatment Phase

The conversion process involves:

  • Surgical placement of 4-6 implant fixtures per arch
  • Healing period of 3-6 months for osseointegration
  • Prosthetic fabrication using digital impressions
  • Final fitting and adjustment of fixed teeth
  • Maintenance instructions and follow-up care

Is This Right for You?

Consider this treatment if you:

  • Currently wear complete dentures
  • Experience instability or discomfort
  • Have dietary restrictions due to dentures
  • Want a permanent fixed solution
  • Have adequate bone volume (confirmed by CBCT)
